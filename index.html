Flagship example — Molecules ↔ Proteins

Why it matters

Decades of experiments capture how small molecules bind to protein targets, but the records are fragmented and inconsistent. Preparing this data unlocks AI models that propose better candidates, speed discovery, and reduce costs.

What we do

  • Unify affinity measures (Kd, Ki, IC50) and units.
  • Map identifiers across sources, deduplicate contradictions.
  • Attach experimental context and assay notes as metadata.
  • Define ML tasks (DTI regression/classification) with fair splits.
  • Document caveats & known biases for responsible use.

AI-ready outputs

tasks:
  - dti_regression: kd_nm
  - dti_classification: binder_threshold_nm
splits:
  - cold_drug
  - cold_target
schema:
  ligand_id: string
  target_uniprot: string
  measure: float
  unit: nM
provenance:
  sources: [literature, db_records]
license: CC BY 4.0
